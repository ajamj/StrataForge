Horizon Picking: Interaction Patterns

How should the user "pick" points on a seismic slice?

🖱️

Point-by-Point

Click individual points on the seismic reflector. High precision, but slower.

🖌️

Continuous Paint

Hold the mouse and "draw" along the reflector. Fast coverage, then simplified into picks.

⚡

Seed & Auto-Track

Click once, and an algorithm automatically follows the peak/trough across the slice.
