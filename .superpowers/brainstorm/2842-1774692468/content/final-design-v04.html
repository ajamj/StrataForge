v0.4 Design Summary: Structural Interpretation

Advanced tools for fault modeling and structural breaks.

Key Features

  • Plane-First Workflow: Interactive fault objects that update in real-time as you pick.
  • Semi-Transparent Planes: Visual feedback allowing you to see through faults to the underlying seismic data.
  • Structural Explorer: Dedicated tree-view for managing fault systems and their associated sticks.
  • RBF Fault Modeling: Adapting the RBF engine to handle near-vertical fault plane interpolation.

Technical Focus

  • Enhancing the sf_render crate to support transparency and depth-sorting for surfaces.
  • Extending the interpretation state to distinguish between Horizons and Faults.

Structural Loop

Define Fault ➔ Pick Sticks ➔ Auto-Update Surface ➔ Verify Alignment (Transparent)
