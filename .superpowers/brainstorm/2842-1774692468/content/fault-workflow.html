Fault Interpretation: Workflow Design

How should we manage fault sticks and planes?

Option A: Stick-Centric
🥖

Pick individual line segments. Group them into a "Fault Plane" manually.

  • Very flexible
  • Good for complex geometry
  • More "click-heavy"
  • Harder to visualize as surface
Option B: Plane-First
🛡️

Create a "Fault Object" first. All picks on that fault automatically update a real-time surface.

  • Fast visualization
  • Intuitive interpretation
  • Automatic modeling can be messy
  • Less control over individual picks
